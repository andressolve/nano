## Character Lock Specifications

### GAIUS (Protagonist)
- Age: 12-13 years old
- Hair: Short dark curly hair
- Skin: Olive Mediterranean complexion
- Eyes: Large expressive dark brown eyes
- Clothing: Simple cream/off-white linen tunic with pale terracotta decorative border
- Build: Slender athletic build
- Status: Middle-class Roman (modest but not poor)

### MARCUS AURELIUS (Philosopher)
- Age: Mid-50s
- Hair: Curly graying hair
- Beard: Full curly graying beard
- Eyes: Tired but kind deep-set eyes
- Face: Weathered philosophical appearance
- Clothing: Simple gray-brown philosopher's robes (NOT imperial purple/gold)
- Demeanor: Humble, dignified, thoughtful
- Posture: Calm, contemplative

## Style Guidelines

- **Art Style**: Painterly, slightly stylized illustration suitable for children's book
- **Historical Period**: Ancient Rome, circa 170 CE
- **Lighting**: Warm dusk/evening tones throughout (golden hour transitioning to twilight)
- **Color Palette**: Warm earth tones, soft golds, terracottas, transitioning to cool purples/blues for night scenes
- **Mood**: Contemplative, peaceful, philosophical
- **Restrictions**: No text, no labels, no modern objects, historically accurate

---

## Technical Notes

- Character reference images were generated first, then used in subsequent prompts via `edit_image` (single character) or `compose_images` (both characters) to maintain consistency
- All prompts specify "no text, no labels" to keep illustrations clean
- Aspect ratio 16:9 specified for landscape-oriented story illustrations
